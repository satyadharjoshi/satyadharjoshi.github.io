### ðŸ”— About viXra.org

**viXra.org** is an open e-print archive founded as an alternative to the traditional preprint server arXiv.org. It provides:

- **Immediate open access** to research papers
- **No endorsement or moderation** of content quality
- **Rapid dissemination** of scientific ideas
- **Coverage across all scientific disciplines**
- **Free submission and access** for all researchers

The name "viXra" is "arXiv" spelled backwards, reflecting its position as an alternative platform for scholarly communication.
